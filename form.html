Submit a Campus Workorder

Request a service from Facilities Management, IMT, Housing Office, Campus Safety, and more.

  • Please do not submit multiple work orders for the same request.
    Please keep one request per work order as different technicians will respond.
    If you have received an email confirmation, we have received your request.
    During heavy work order seasons, requests are not necessarily prioritized by date.
    Housing furniture requests may take up to two weeks at the start of semesters.
    For after business hours maintenance emergencies, contact Campus Safety at x3898.
